About Us

We are a fourth generation local company. We employ more than 40 people, at our mill on Chapples Island as well as at our logging site(s) at Millertown, Nl.

Ralph Philpot

Cottle's Island Lumber co Ltd was founded by Ralph Philpot in 1969

Rex Philpot

Mr Rex Philpot (left) continued in his fathers foot steps and built a thriving business, that employs 40 local people. We have expanded our operations to include reusing the by-product sawdust and creating a premium high quality wood burning pellet at our Pellet Facility.

Lori Philpot

Lori, learning from her father (Rex) becoming a strong leader in the lumber business.

Robin Philpot
